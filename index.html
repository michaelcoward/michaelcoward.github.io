Project Idea

Overview

My idea is to create a single player, linear, 2D platforming action game using the Unity engine with a pixel art style. The concept of the game involves starting the game playing as a lowly unidentifiable creature/animal that would belong somewhere close to the bottom of the food chain and progressively work through levels of platforming that become more difficult as you progress. At the end of each level, there will be a boss encounter of a slightly more advanced form of animal life. Upon defeating the boss encounter, your unidentifiable creature will gain a trait of the animal it defeats and be able to apply those newfound traits to help it navigate the evolving challenges of the environment you are trying to traverse as well as help you defeat new more challenging boss encounters. The goal of the game is to become the ultimate life-form.

Motivation

My motivation mainly stems from nostalgia and ideas I had as a kid for what would be a fun and exciting game to play. Some of my inspiration draws from a platforming flash-game I played as a kid called ‘Monster Evolution’ and one of my all-time favourite games through childhood, Spore. Games that are centred on evolution being delivered in an exciting way have always been a huge love of mine, and as such, with this game I could deliver this experience through a unique filter and give many kids that memorable experience that I enjoyed so much when I was younger.

Description

The game will involve a set of linear levels where you cannot progress until you have completed the level you are currently on. However, as the creature you play as becomes more advanced and has more traits at it’s disposal, the ways in which you can traverse and solve problems within the environment can become a choice. An example would be utlising the traits of a rat that can pass through tight spaces in a forest or using the traits of a monkey to climb to high places to get to different platforms.

Tools and Technologies

The Unity engine is of course required as a popular platform for indie games. The beginners package for Unity is free however it’s capabilities are limited and a monetised UnityPlus or UnityPro package may become required as the needs of the project become more clear. Some form of graphic design technology/program may also be required such as photo shop to create concept art before being integrated in to the game. My knowledge of what is needed to create an indie videogame is limited as I am yet to go through the process and lack any experience in this area.
